# Future Roadmap: Advanced Legal Research Platform

## 1. Research Capabilities
- **Jurisdictional Expansion**: Add comprehensive support for UK, Canada, Australia, and EU law with jurisdiction-specific specialized agents.
- **Real-time Legislative Tracking**: Integration with live parliamentary/congressional feeds to track bills in progress and their potential impact on current cases.
- **Shepardizing/KeyCite Equivalent**: Automated verification of whether cited cases are still good law (overturned, affirmed, etc.).
- **Judge Analytics**: Analysis of specific judges' ruling history, tendencies, and citation patterns to tailor arguments.
- **Cross-Lingual Precedent Mining**: Seamlessly search legal databases in foreign languages (e.g., Spanish, Mandarin) and return translated summaries relevant to international disputes.

## 2. Integrated Components
- **Drafting Assistant**: A "Copilot" for legal drafting that suggests clauses, arguments, and citations within the document editor.
- **Discovery Automation**: Tools to ingest terabytes of discovery documents, cluster them by topic, and identify "smoking gun" evidence using semantic search.
- **Visual Argument Builder**: A drag-and-drop interface to build legal arguments visually, linking facts to evidence to law.
- **Smart Calendar & Docketing**: Auto-calculation of filing deadlines based on court rules, with automated reminders.
- **Automated Document Assembly**: Instantly generate routine complaints, subpoenas, and motions by pulling from client fact databases and local court templates.

## 3. User Experience & Interface
- **Split-Screen Deep Dive**: When reading a case, allow "peeking" into cited cases in a side panel without losing context.
- **Interactive Timeline**: Auto-generated timeline of facts from case documents, editable and linkable to evidence.
- **Network Graph Visualization**: Visual graph showing relationships between parties, cases, and statutes.
- **Voice-Activated Research**: "Hey Nyaya, find cases related to..." for hands-free research during drafting.
- **VR/AR Courtroom Prep**: Virtual depositions and moot court experiences powered by AI acting as the opposing counsel or judge to test lines of questioning.

## 4. Collaboration & Enterprise
- **Real-time Multiplayer**: Google Docs-style collaboration on case files and research notes.
- **Knowledge Management**: "Firm Brain" that indexes all past work product of the firm for internal reuse.
- **Client Portal**: Secure view for clients to see case status, upcoming dates, and approved documents.
- **Automated Conflict Checking**: Instantly cross-reference new prospective clients against the "Firm Brain" to detect ethical conflicts of interest.

## 5. Security & Evidence Integrity
- **Blockchain Chain of Custody**: Immutable, verifiable logging of when digital evidence was uploaded, accessed, and modified to ensure its validity in trials.
- **Deepfake & Forgery Detection**: Integrated computer vision models to scientifically analyze uploaded images/videos/audio to detect tampering, synthetic generation, or hidden metadata edits.
- **Redaction Intelligence**: AI that auto-detects PII, financial details, and trade secrets in thousand-page document batches and applies reversible or fixed redactions prior to opposing counsel discovery.

## 6. Predictive Analytics
- **Win Probability Scoring**: Machine learning models that analyze the facts against large corpuses of similar historical case outcomes to generate a win-probability percentage.
- **Settlement Calculator**: AI-driven analysis of historical damages, jury awards, and settlements inside specific jurisdictions to recommend optimal negotiation floors and ceilings.
- **Opposing Counsel Profiling**: Aggregated statistics and historical strategies of the opposing firm or lawyer based on public dockets and rulings.
